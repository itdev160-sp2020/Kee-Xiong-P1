About Us

Highlander Home Health Care, LLC is a freestanding Personal Care Agency licensed in the State of Wisconsin. It was founded by Richard Chao Vang in 2005 to provide personal care services to the communities in the Milwaukee and surrounding counties. Highlander services people of all culture and has many multilingual professional staff to help those who may have a language barrier.

A Message from Our Founder

Thank you so much for visiting Highlander Home Health Care. I, as the founder of Highlander Home Health Care and your provider, believe that home health care is a progressive alternative to institutionalization. Therefore, I focus on providing a full range of quality, cost-effective health care services to patients within our services coverage area. I also believe patients and families should be treated with respect while providing care to promote wellness. I recognize and respect the autonomy of our patients and families when making notes affecting their health status. It is my duty to make sure our services meet your health care needs, and that we provide good customer service to all our patients and networks. Your feedback and recommendations are welcome. Please stop by and let me know or email me at email@gmatc/matc.edu. I would really appreciate your comments.
